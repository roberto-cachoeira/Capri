Shrimp Salad

Ingredients

  • Peeled and deveined shrimp without heads
  • Kani, cut diagonally into 3cm pieces
  • Sliced onion (preferably red onion)
  • Red bell pepper, cut into thin strips
  • Chopped cilantro
  • Juice of 2 Sicilian lemons
  • 3 or 4 tablespoons of high-quality olive oil

Instructions

  1. If the shrimp are not already cooked, quickly saut√© them with your favorite seasonings until they change color and are just right.
  2. Shred the diagonally cut Kani pieces slightly, ensuring they remain large pieces to resemble shredded crab.
  3. Place the Kani and shrimp in a large bowl.
  4. Add the sliced onion, red bell pepper, cilantro, lemon juice, and olive oil to the bowl.
  5. For a less pungent taste, you may pour hot water over the onions before adding them to the salad.
  6. Refrigerate the salad for 1 hour before serving for enhanced flavor.
