Brazilian Chicken and Rice Soup

Ingredients

  • 1 1/2 yellow onion diced
  • 5 carrots chopped in small pieces
  • 1 tsp salt
  • A pinch of freshly ground black pepper
  • 1/4 tsp dried rosemary
  • 1/4 tsp ground celery seeds
  • 3 garlic cloves minced
  • 1 whole rotisserie chicken shredded (optional, can use fresh chicken thighs with bones, no skin)
  • 1 cup rice
  • 7 cups boiling water
  • 2 tsp better than bouillon roasted chicken base
  • 1 tsp better than bouillon roasted garlic base
  • 1 dried bay leaf
  • 3 gold potatoes medium diced
  • 2 whisked eggs
  • 1/2 cup chopped fresh parsley

Instructions

  1. Sauté the onions and carrots with salt, black pepper, rosemary, celery seeds, and garlic until carrots are almost tender.
  2. Add shredded chicken and combine well.
  3. Stir in the rice and let it sauté for a bit, stirring constantly to prevent sticking.
  4. Add the potatoes, boiling water, chicken and garlic bouillon, and bay leaf.
  5. Simmer until potatoes are tender.
  6. Add the whisked eggs into the simmering soup, stirring well to cook the eggs. Top with parsley.
  7. Serve in a bowl with toast and enjoy!
